Article memory clues

der

Often male people, days, months, seasons, many words ending in er, en, el.

die

Often female people, words ending in ung, heit, keit, schaft, ion, ei, in.

das

Often diminutives ending in chen or lein, many infinitive nouns, many words ending in ment or um.

These are clues, not laws. German articles like to act innocent then betray you, so memorize the full noun with article.
